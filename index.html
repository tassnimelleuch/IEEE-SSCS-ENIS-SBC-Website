IEEE Solid State Circuit Society chapter

The IEEE Solid State Circuit Society (SSCS) chapter is one of ten technical chapters of IEEE ENIS SB. It was established on 25 June 2022 with only 7 members. Today, it has over 100 students passionate about ICs and electronics.

It is a chapter that focuses on fabricated integrated circuit IC designs in contrast to simulate circuits and analyze models for all applications using relevant materials and interconnections and make a printed circuit board PCB.

SSCS Chapter - IEEE Enis Student Branch Goals

The SSCS (Solid-State Circuit Society) Student Chapter at ENIS aims to:

  • Promote microelectronics for engineering students and master students and to bridge the gap between academia and industry.
  • Focuses on fabricated integrated circuit designs (chips) for all applications using relevant materials and interconnections.
  • Offer workshops, competitions, communication channels, recognition, leadership opportunities, and networking to our members.

Join us to advance your skills and connect with the future of technology!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is IEEE ?

IEEE is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.

What is ENIS??

Founded in 1983, the National School of Engineers of Sfax (ENIS) is one of the major institutions for education, research, and technological transfer. It provides training for engineers in seven disciplines, namely electrical engineering, electromechanical engineering, computer engineering, materials engineering, civil engineering, biological engineering, and geo-resources and environmental engineering.

How many Chapters/Affinity Groups are there in IEEE ENIS Student Branch?

IEEE ENIS Student branch contains 11 chapters ,1 affinity group and 1 unit.

Can all students from different fields join IEEE ENIS Student Branch?

Yes, the IEEE ENIS Student Branch contains diverse chapters that cover all the present fields at ENIS.
